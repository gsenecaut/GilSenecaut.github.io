About Me

Name: Gil Teixeira de Sousa Senecaut   -   Nationality: French, Portuguese

I was born and raised in Macau, China, where I did my IGCSEs before moving to Oxford, United Kingdom, to complete my secondary education. Studying in a highly diverse international environment at St. Clare's allowed me to explore my passions for Mathematics and Economics. I decided to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics with Economics, with the intention of keeping my options open and specializing with a Master's degree later on.

As I approached the end of my Bachelor's degree at the University of Sussex in Brighton, I became increasingly interested in data analytics and programming languages such as python and R. Looking into career paths in Data, I discovered that it could be the ideal fit for me. Therefore, I enrolled in a Master's degree program in Big Data Analytics at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid to further develop my skills in this field. It was during this program that I acquired the necessary skills and tools to make a meaningful contribution as a Data Analyst.

After my Master's program, I had the privilege of completing a year-long internship with the FCG team at Ageas, where I played a crucial role in developing a robust database and transitioning to dynamic reporting with Power BI. This experience not only sharpened my technical skills but also taught me valuable lessons in project management, teamwork, and communication. It solidified my passion for data analytics and reinforced my career aspirations in this dynamic field.
